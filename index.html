About Me

I am a researcher at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the University of Athens, hosted by Aggelos Kiayias. I'm in the process of obtaining a PhD from the University of Edinburgh, and hold a Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens. My research interests are in Distributed Protocols and Cryptography, with a special focus on Blockchain Protocols, Byzantine Agreement.
