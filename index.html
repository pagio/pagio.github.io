About Me

I am a researcher at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the University of Athens. I obtained my PhD from the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Aggelos Kiayias, and hold a Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens. My research is about Distributed Protocols and Cryptography, with a special focus on Blockchain Protocols and Byzantine Agreement.
